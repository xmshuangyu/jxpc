Statements Regarding the Fraudulent Use of the HCC Employees’ Identity to Conduct Business Online
Print
Write by Home Credit 2014-05-12
Recently, some bloggers on the Weibo and Baidu Tieba claim to be our employees, and can help handle the unpaid loan and erase credit records. In return, they require a certain amount of agency fee. This behavior is not only misleading consumers, but also hurts our corporate reputation and interests. We solemnly declare here: these intermediaries do not have any relations with us. If any cash fraud is involved, we shall reserve the rights to conduct investigation for their legal responsibility.

Meanwhile, in order to avoid more losses, we remind consumers:

1. No one in HCC can change or delete the credit record of customers.

2. For future product offers, we suggest the customer to maintain a good credit record with HCC and other lenders . Such a record

created by repayment discipline like timely and fully loan repayment or not and cannot be changed retrospectively. Therefore, we encourage consumers to repay the loan according to the contract.

3. Currently, HCC is connected to the PBOC Financial Credit Information Database and Pengyuan Credit Information System which are legally established credit information systems. PBOC regulates and controls the usage of the records. As there is more and more credit information providers in China today, HCC will cooperate with more registers and provide them with historical credit record in the future.

4. All fees charged of the loan product promoted by HCC are stated in itemized format in the contract. There are no other fees collected during the application process.

5. Please protect your personal information such as ID card. Don’t share it and let other people to use it.

6. If you have any questions or concerns about any part of the sales process or the behavior of HCC sales representative when applying for the loan, please contact us according to below email or hotline numbers, or send private message to our official Sina Weibo account @捷信金融官方微博.
