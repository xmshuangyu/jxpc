Introduction

Headquartered in Shenzhen, Home Credit China is an innovative in-shop payment provider, enabling almost every shop customer with regular income to use installment service directly in the shop with approval time within 60 minutes to buy the goods he or she wishes to have and spread the repayment over several months.
