  • About product

    What is the Cash Loan?
    What is the Cash Loan? Cash loan is dedicated to have continuity wage income people issued, with its monthly wage income as the main basis of the loan judgments, for small loans personal consumption. The human Chinese loan loan products Foreign Trade Trust Company Limited, or Gitzo consumer finance company limited, please refer to the consumer credit contract.
    How much is the loan amount and duration of the loan?
    The loan amount of 5000 yuan to 40000 yuan, to meet your family decoration, personal training, travel, shopping and other personal consumption required. The loan period of 12,18,24,30,36 months optional flexible.
    If the minimum repayment period is 12 months, Can I pay off the loan within 12 times, such as 4 or 6 times?
    Sure. You can select any of the loan period, repayment in advance in the selection you want to repay.
    Do I meet for the Cash loan conditions?
    Sure. You can select any of the loan period, repayment in advance in the selection you want to repay.
