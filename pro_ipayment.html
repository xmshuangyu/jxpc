Installment payment shopping business

POS (Point of sales) loan is the major product provided by Home Credit in China. POS loan is unsecured loan which is provided for customers to finance purchase of various types of their favorite durable goods, such as cell phone, computer, motorbike, home appliance and so on, customers can apply the installment service directly in shops.The key advantages of our product are simple, fast and flexible:
