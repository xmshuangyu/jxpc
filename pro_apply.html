Applicant requirement

Chinese citizens with ID card (excluding Chinese with foreign passport, citizens of Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan, and military units)
Age range

18-55

Document requirement

1, ID card.

2, Second document (any of the following):

a, bank card   b, hukou   c, driving license   d, bank transaction slip   e, bank book   f, employee badge

Application process:

PROCESS
  • Customer selects his/her favorite goods and applies for POS loan service directly in the retailer shop which cooperates with Home Credit company;
    1
  • Sales representative checks customer’s documents and help to fill-in application form, approval process takes less than 60 minutes;
    1
  • If approved customer signs the loan contract and pays payment. Customer gets goods from retailer;
    1
  • Customer pays monthly installments according to instructions in “Repayment Guidance”.
